Prime Graph Labelings

During the 2018-2019 academic school year, I was fortunate to be part of a multi-campus team between Centre College and Murray State University researching graph labelings thanks to a National Science Foundation CURM grant (DMS-1722563).
My team, consisting of another Centre student and a student from Murray State University, compiled our results on the prime labelings of snake graphs into an article published with the PUMP Undergraduate Journal of Mathematics in August of 2019.
Additionally, another paper detailing the undergraduate research experience was published in the Math Horizons journal in January of 2020.
Both journal articles are available upon request.
